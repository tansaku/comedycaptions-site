Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

ComedyCaptions is designed for personal use and collects minimal information necessary for operation:

  • Video Content: Videos you upload for processing are temporarily stored during analysis and automatically deleted after processing
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about app usage to improve performance
  • Account Information: Basic account details if you create an account

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Process your videos and generate captions and clips
  • Improve the AI algorithms
  • Provide technical support
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is paramount:

  • Videos are processed locally when possible
  • Any temporarily uploaded content is deleted within 24 hours
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • No video content is stored permanently on our servers

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information or video content with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Third-Party Services

ComedyCaptions may integrate with:

  • TikTok's API for posting capabilities
  • Cloud storage services for temporary processing
  • Analytics services for usage statistics

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Access information we have about you
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

ComedyCaptions is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
